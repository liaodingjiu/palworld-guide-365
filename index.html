❓ Quick Answers

Common Palworld questions — straight answers, no fluff.

How many bases can I have in Palworld?
You start with 1 base. At Base Level 10, you unlock a second. At Base Level 15, you unlock a third — 3 bases total in the base game. You can unlock more by tweaking server settings.
What's the best Pal for combat?
Jetragon is #1 overall — fastest flying mount, devastating Dragon attacks, and a missile launcher partner skill. For early-mid game, breed yourself an Anubis. See full tier list →
How does breeding work in Palworld?
Each Pal has a hidden breeding power value. Breed two Pals and the game averages their values, producing the Pal closest to that average. You need a Breeding Farm (unlocked at level 19) and Cake. Full breeding guide →
Where should I build my first base?
Don't build right where you spawn. Walk inland to flat, open ground near resources. Verdant Brook (-257, 201) is our top pick for starter bases — it has ore, wood, stone, and Paldium nearby. All base locations →
