About PalGuide

PalGuide is built and maintained by The Pal Master, a Palworld player with 500+ hours in the game. I started this site because I was tired of scattered, outdated, and AI-generated resources that copied each other without verifying anything. Every guide on PalGuide is tested in my own playthroughs — I play, I verify, I write.

How We Verify Our Guides

All content on PalGuide goes through a three-step verification process:

  • In-game testing — strategies, locations, and mechanics are tested firsthand
  • Cross-referenced with the Palworld Wiki and official Pocketpair patch notes
  • Community-verified — data is checked against the r/Palworld community

Update Policy

Palworld gets regular patches and content updates. PalGuide content is updated within 48 hours of every major patch. The last site-wide update was July 10, 2026, covering the Palworld 1.0 release and new server guide.
