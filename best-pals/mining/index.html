Best Mining Pals in Palworld 2026

Mining is the most critical part of base automation. The right mining Pal can increase your ore and ingot output by 3-5x. This ranking considers mining level, work efficiency, movement speed, and accessibility.

How We Rank

Our rankings combine in-game data with hands-on gameplay experience. Each Pal is evaluated on:

  • Work Efficiency — work suitability level (higher = faster resource output)
  • Movement Speed — affects actual work rate (less time walking = more time working)
  • Accessibility — how early and easily you can obtain this Pal
  • Food Efficiency — lower food drain means more uptime

Scores are expert-assessed based on actual gameplay testing. Only Pals withmining ≥ 2 are included.
