❓ Frequently Asked Questions — Killamari Primo

How do I get Killamari Primo in Palworld?
Killamari Primo can be caught in the wild. It can also be obtained through breeding using the standard breeding power formula (BP 1000).
What is Killamari Primo best used for?
Killamari Primo excels at transporting (Lv 2), watering (Lv 1), Gathering (Lv 1). Its Neutral typing and stat distribution (ATK 60) make it a strong choice for transporting tasks.
Can you ride Killamari Primo?
No, Killamari Primo cannot be ridden. However, it can still be assigned to your base as a worker or added to your party — its strength lies in transporting work (Lv 2) rather than transportation.
What does Killamari Primo's Partner Skill do?
Killamari Primo's Partner Skill is Fried Killamari: While in team, modifies the performance of the equipped glider. Enables gentle floating while gliding for an extended time.
Is Killamari Primo good for transporting?
It's decent. Killamari Primo has transporting Lv 2. Serviceable for transporting if you don't have higher-tier alternatives yet.
