❓ Frequently Asked Questions — Munchill

How do I get Munchill in Palworld?
Munchill can be caught in the wild. It can also be obtained through breeding using the standard breeding power formula (BP 1000).
What is Munchill best used for?
Munchill excels at cooling (Lv 2), watering (Lv 1), transporting (Lv 1). Its Neutral typing and stat distribution (ATK 80) make it a strong choice for cooling tasks.
Can you ride Munchill?
No, Munchill cannot be ridden. However, it can still be assigned to your base as a worker or added to your party — its strength lies in cooling work (Lv 2) rather than transportation.
What does Munchill's Partner Skill do?
Munchill's Partner Skill is Icy Maw: While in team, Munchill cools ingredients inside its mouth, making the food in your inventory less likely to spoil.
Is Munchill good for cooling?
It's decent. Munchill has cooling Lv 2. Serviceable for cooling if you don't have higher-tier alternatives yet.
