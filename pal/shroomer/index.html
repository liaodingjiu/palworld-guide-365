❓ Frequently Asked Questions — Shroomer

How do I get Shroomer in Palworld?
Shroomer can be caught in the wild. It can also be obtained through breeding using the standard breeding power formula (BP 720).
What is Shroomer best used for?
Shroomer excels at planting (Lv 2), Gathering (Lv 2), lumbering (Lv 2). Its Grass typing and stat distribution (ATK 80) make it a strong choice for planting tasks.
Can you ride Shroomer?
Yes, Shroomer can be ridden as a ground mount. Craft its saddle at a Pal Gear Workbench. While not a flyer, it provides solid ground mobility.
What does Shroomer's Partner Skill do?
Shroomer's Partner Skill is Swooshy Spores: Can be ridden. While at a base, its mysterious spores slow the rate at which the SAN value of allies at the base decreases.
Is Shroomer good for planting?
It's decent. Shroomer has planting Lv 2. Serviceable for planting if you don't have higher-tier alternatives yet.
