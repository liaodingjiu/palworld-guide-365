Panthalus is a Water-type Legendary Pal (#203) and one of the best flying mounts in Palworld 1.0. It does not spawn in the wild — you must complete a quest chain starting with defeating tower boss Auri, collecting four giant bone remains, crafting the Echoing Flute, and summoning it as a Level 70 boss (~350,000 HP). Bring strong Electric-type Pals, and make sure to catch it — killing forces a redo. Unlike most Pals, Panthalus has zero normal work suitability. Its value is pure: the best flying mount in the game, plus a unique base defense patrol ability via its Partner Skill Resonant Guardian.

Partner Skill — Resonant Guardian

Effect: Allows you to ride Panthalus as a flying mount. When assigned to a base, Panthalus patrols the skies and bombards intruders — it doesn't do standard work tasks, it functions as an aerial defense turret.

This makes Panthalus unique: no other Pal combines top-tier flying mobility with automated base defense. If you care about raid protection, assigning Panthalus to your base is a strong defensive layer.

📋 How to Get Panthalus — Step Checklist

  1. Defeat Auri (tower boss) — this unlocks the Panthalus quest line.
  2. Collect 4 giant bone remains — scattered across the map. Check your quest log for markers.
  3. Craft the Echoing Flute — required to summon Panthalus. Gather the materials beforehand.
  4. Go to the shrine on the Islet — use the Echoing Flute to summon Panthalus.
  5. Bring Electric-type Pals (level 55+) — Panthalus is Water-type, weak to Electric. The fight has ~350,000 HP at level 70.
  6. CATCH it — do NOT kill. If you kill Panthalus, you have to redo the entire summoning fight.
  7. Bring the caught Panthalus to the World Tree terminal — this opens the gate to progress the main story.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get Panthalus in Palworld?
Panthalus is quest-exclusive. Steps: 1) Defeat tower boss Auri, 2) Collect 4 giant bone remains, 3) Craft the Echoing Flute, 4) Use it at the Islet shrine to summon the level 70 boss (~350,000 HP), 5) Defeat and catch it. Killing it forces you to restart the fight.
What type is Panthalus and what is it weak to?
Panthalus is a pure Water-type Legendary (Paldeck #203). It is weak to Electric attacks — bring strong Electric Pals like Orserk or Grizzbolt for the boss fight.
Is Panthalus a good flying mount?
Yes — one of the best in the game. Its Partner Skill Resonant Guardian lets you ride it as a flying mount. It also patrols your base from above and bombards intruders, making it useful both for exploration and base defense.
Can Panthalus do base work like Mining or Handiwork?
No. Panthalus has zero normal work suitability. It only provides aerial defense via its Partner Skill — it patrols and bombards raiders. For mining, crafting, or other base tasks, use other Pals.
What level should I be to fight Panthalus?
Panthalus is a level 70 boss with ~350,000 HP. Bring level 55+ Electric-type Pals. This is endgame content — you need to have progressed through the main story and defeated Auri first.
What does Panthalus drop?
Panthalus drops Diamond and Giant Pal Soul. You also receive 5 Ancient Technology Points as a quest encounter reward.
Can I breed Panthalus instead of doing the quest?
No. Panthalus is quest-exclusive — it does not spawn in the wild and cannot be bred from other Pals. The only way to get it is completing the Echoing Flute quest chain.
Is Panthalus required for the main story?
Yes. After catching Panthalus, bring it to the World Tree terminal to open the gate and progress the main quest line. You can't skip this step.
