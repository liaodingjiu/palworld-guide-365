❓ Frequently Asked Questions — Lovander

How do I get Lovander in Palworld?
Lovander can be caught in the wild. It can also be obtained through breeding using the standard breeding power formula (BP 940).
What is Lovander best used for?
Lovander excels at Handiwork (Lv 2), medicine (Lv 2), transporting (Lv 2). Its Neutral typing and stat distribution (ATK 70) make it a strong choice for handiwork tasks.
Can you ride Lovander?
No, Lovander cannot be ridden. However, it can still be assigned to your base as a worker or added to your party — its strength lies in handiwork work (Lv 2) rather than transportation.
What does Lovander's Partner Skill do?
Lovander's Partner Skill is Heart Drain: While fighting together, grants the player and Lovander the life steal effect which absorbs some of the received damage and restores Health.
Is Lovander good for handiwork?
It's decent. Lovander has Handiwork Lv 2. Serviceable for handiwork if you don't have higher-tier alternatives yet.
