❓ Frequently Asked Questions — Fuack

How do I get Fuack in Palworld?
Fuack can be caught in the wild. It can also be obtained through breeding using the standard breeding power formula (BP 1330).
What is Fuack best used for?
Fuack excels at watering (Lv 1), Handiwork (Lv 1), transporting (Lv 1). Its Water typing and stat distribution (ATK 80) make it a strong choice for watering tasks.
Can you ride Fuack?
No, Fuack cannot be ridden. However, it can still be assigned to your base as a worker or added to your party — its strength lies in watering work (Lv 1) rather than transportation.
What does Fuack's Partner Skill do?
Fuack's Partner Skill is Surfing Slam: When activated, Fuack body surfs towards an enemy and slams into them.
Is Fuack good for watering?
Fuack has watering Lv 1 — basic capability. It can do the job in a pinch, but there are much stronger options for dedicated watering work.
