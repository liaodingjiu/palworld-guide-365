❓ Frequently Asked Questions — Faleris

How do I get Faleris in Palworld?
Faleris can be caught in the wild. It can also be obtained through breeding using the standard breeding power formula (BP 370).
What is Faleris best used for?
Faleris excels at kindling (Lv 3), transporting (Lv 3). Its Fire typing and stat distribution (ATK 105) make it a strong choice for kindling tasks.
Can you ride Faleris?
Yes, Faleris can be ridden as a flying mount! You'll need to craft Faleris's saddle at a Pal Gear Workbench. Flying mounts let you traverse the map quickly and engage in aerial combat.
What does Faleris's Partner Skill do?
Faleris's Partner Skill is Scorching Predator: Can be ridden as a flying mount. While fighting together, Ice Pals drop more items when defeated.
Is Faleris good for kindling?
Yes, a very strong choice. Faleris has kindling Lv 3. It's a reliable, high-efficiency worker for kindling — excellent from mid-game onward.
