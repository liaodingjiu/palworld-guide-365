❓ Frequently Asked Questions — Tanzee

How do I get Tanzee in Palworld?
Tanzee can be caught in the wild. It can also be obtained through breeding using the standard breeding power formula (BP 1250).
What is Tanzee best used for?
Tanzee excels at planting (Lv 1), Handiwork (Lv 1), Gathering (Lv 1). Its Grass typing and stat distribution (ATK 70) make it a strong choice for planting tasks.
Can you ride Tanzee?
No, Tanzee cannot be ridden. However, it can still be assigned to your base as a worker or added to your party — its strength lies in planting work (Lv 1) rather than transportation.
What does Tanzee's Partner Skill do?
Tanzee's Partner Skill is Cheery Rifle: When activated, Tanzee will mercilessly fire an assault rifle at nearby enemies for a limited time.
Is Tanzee good for planting?
Tanzee has planting Lv 1 — basic capability. It can do the job in a pinch, but there are much stronger options for dedicated planting work.
