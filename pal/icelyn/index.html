❓ Frequently Asked Questions — Icelyn

How do I get Icelyn in Palworld?
Icelyn can be caught in the wild. It can also be obtained through breeding using the standard breeding power formula (BP 1000).
What is Icelyn best used for?
Icelyn excels at cooling (Lv 3), Handiwork (Lv 2), medicine (Lv 2). Its Ice typing and stat distribution (ATK 115) make it a strong choice for cooling tasks.
Can you ride Icelyn?
No, Icelyn cannot be ridden. However, it can still be assigned to your base as a worker or added to your party — its strength lies in cooling work (Lv 3) rather than transportation.
What does Icelyn's Partner Skill do?
Icelyn's Partner Skill is Witch's Icy Veil: While fighting together, changes the player's attack type to Ice.
Is Icelyn good for cooling?
Yes, a very strong choice. Icelyn has cooling Lv 3. It's a reliable, high-efficiency worker for cooling — excellent from mid-game onward.
