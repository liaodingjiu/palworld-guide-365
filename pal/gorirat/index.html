How do I get Gorirat in Palworld?
Gorirat can be caught in the wild. It can also be obtained through breeding using the standard breeding power formula (BP 1040).
What is Gorirat best used for?
Gorirat excels at transporting (Lv 3), lumbering (Lv 2), Handiwork (Lv 1). Its Neutral typing and stat distribution (ATK 95) make it a strong choice for transporting tasks.
Can you ride Gorirat?
No, Gorirat cannot be ridden. However, it can still be assigned to your base as a worker or added to your party — its strength lies in transporting work (Lv 3) rather than transportation.
What does Gorirat's Partner Skill do?
Gorirat's Partner Skill is Full-Power Gorilla Mode: When activated, unleashes a primal fury that increases Gorirat's Attack for a limited time.
Is Gorirat good for transporting?
Yes, a very strong choice. Gorirat has transporting Lv 3. It's a reliable, high-efficiency worker for transporting — excellent from mid-game onward.
