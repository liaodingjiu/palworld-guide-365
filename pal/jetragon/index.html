❓ Frequently Asked Questions — Jetragon

How do I get Jetragon in Palworld?
Jetragon can be caught in the wild. A field boss spawns at Jetragon's Domain. It can also be obtained through breeding.
What is Jetragon best used for?
Jetragon is best used for Gathering (Lv 3). Focus on this role to get the most value from Jetragon.
Can you ride Jetragon?
Yes, Jetragon can be ridden as a flying mount! You'll need to craft Jetragon's saddle at a Pal Gear Workbench. Flying mounts let you traverse the map quickly and engage in aerial combat.
What does Jetragon's Partner Skill do?

Is Jetragon good for gathering?
Yes, a very strong choice. Jetragon has Gathering Lv 3. It's a reliable, high-efficiency worker for gathering — excellent from mid-game onward.
