❓ Frequently Asked Questions — Necromus

How do I get Necromus in Palworld?
Necromus can be caught in the wild. A field boss spawns at Necromus's Domain. It can also be obtained through breeding.
What is Necromus best used for?
Necromus excels at lumbering (Lv 2), Mining (Lv 2). Its Dark typing and stat distribution (ATK 145) make it a strong choice for lumbering tasks.
Can you ride Necromus?
Yes, Necromus can be ridden as a ground mount. Craft its saddle at a Pal Gear Workbench. While not a flyer, it provides solid ground mobility.
What does Necromus's Partner Skill do?
Necromus's Partner Skill is Dark Knight of the Abyss: Can be ridden. Can double jump while mounted.
Is Necromus good for lumbering?
It's decent. Necromus has lumbering Lv 2. Serviceable for lumbering if you don't have higher-tier alternatives yet.
