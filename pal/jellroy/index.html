❓ Frequently Asked Questions — Jellroy

How do I get Jellroy in Palworld?
Jellroy can be caught in the wild. It can also be obtained through breeding using the standard breeding power formula (BP 1000).
What is Jellroy best used for?
Jellroy excels at watering (Lv 2), Handiwork (Lv 1), medicine (Lv 1). Its Neutral typing and stat distribution (ATK 75) make it a strong choice for watering tasks.
Can you ride Jellroy?
No, Jellroy cannot be ridden. However, it can still be assigned to your base as a worker or added to your party — its strength lies in watering work (Lv 2) rather than transportation.
What does Jellroy's Partner Skill do?
Jellroy's Partner Skill is Jellroy Drop: While in team, increases items obtained from salvage. While Jelliette and Jellroy are in your base, Jellroy's watering speed is increased.
Is Jellroy good for watering?
It's decent. Jellroy has watering Lv 2. Serviceable for watering if you don't have higher-tier alternatives yet.
