How do I get Lyleen in Palworld?

What is Lyleen best used for?
Lyleen excels at planting (Lv 4), Handiwork (Lv 3), medicine (Lv 3). Its Grass typing and stat distribution (ATK 110) make it a strong choice for planting tasks.

No, Lyleen cannot be ridden. However, it can still be assigned to your base as a worker or added to your party — its strength lies in planting work (Lv 4) rather than transportation.

Is Lyleen good for planting?
Yes, absolutely. Lyleen has planting Lv 4 — top-tier efficiency for this role. It's one of the best Pals you can assign for planting tasks.
