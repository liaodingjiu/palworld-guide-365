❓ Frequently Asked Questions — Turtacle

How do I get Turtacle in Palworld?
Turtacle can be caught in the wild. It can also be obtained through breeding using the standard breeding power formula (BP 1000).
What is Turtacle best used for?
Turtacle excels at watering (Lv 2), Mining (Lv 1), transporting (Lv 1). Its Water typing and stat distribution (ATK 75) make it a strong choice for watering tasks.
Can you ride Turtacle?
No, Turtacle cannot be ridden. However, it can still be assigned to your base as a worker or added to your party — its strength lies in watering work (Lv 2) rather than transportation.
What does Turtacle's Partner Skill do?
Turtacle's Partner Skill is Spikey Carrier: While in team, Turtacle helps carry supplies, decreasing the weight of armor in the player's inventory.
Is Turtacle good for watering?
It's decent. Turtacle has watering Lv 2. Serviceable for watering if you don't have higher-tier alternatives yet.
