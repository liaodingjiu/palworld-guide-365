❓ Frequently Asked Questions — Braloha

How do I get Braloha in Palworld?
Braloha can be caught in the wild. It can also be obtained through breeding using the standard breeding power formula (BP 1000).
What is Braloha best used for?
Braloha excels at planting (Lv 4), Gathering (Lv 4), Mining (Lv 2). Its Neutral typing and stat distribution (ATK 95) make it a strong choice for planting tasks.
Can you ride Braloha?
Yes, Braloha can be ridden as a ground mount. Craft its saddle at a Pal Gear Workbench. While not a flyer, it provides solid ground mobility.
What does Braloha's Partner Skill do?
Braloha's Partner Skill is Balmy Weather: Can be ridden. While at a base, shortens the time it takes Pals assigned to BreedFarm to produce eggs. (Effect does not stack.)
Is Braloha good for planting?
Yes, absolutely. Braloha has planting Lv 4 — top-tier efficiency for this role. It's one of the best Pals you can assign for planting tasks.
