❓ Frequently Asked Questions — Ghangler

How do I get Ghangler in Palworld?
Ghangler can be caught in the wild. It can also be obtained through breeding using the standard breeding power formula (BP 1000).
What is Ghangler best used for?
Ghangler excels at watering (Lv 4), transporting (Lv 2). Its Neutral typing and stat distribution (ATK 125) make it a strong choice for watering tasks.
Can you ride Ghangler?
Yes, Ghangler can be ridden as a ground mount. Craft its saddle at a Pal Gear Workbench. While not a flyer, it provides solid ground mobility.
What does Ghangler's Partner Skill do?
Ghangler's Partner Skill is Master Night Angler: Can be ridden to travel quickly across water. While fighting together, the time it takes for Pals to bite during night fishing is reduced.
Is Ghangler good for watering?
Yes, absolutely. Ghangler has watering Lv 4 — top-tier efficiency for this role. It's one of the best Pals you can assign for watering tasks.
