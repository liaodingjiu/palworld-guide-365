❓ Frequently Asked Questions — Depresso

How do I get Depresso in Palworld?
Depresso can be caught in the wild. It can also be obtained through breeding using the standard breeding power formula (BP 1380).
What is Depresso best used for?
Depresso excels at Handiwork (Lv 1), Mining (Lv 1), transporting (Lv 1). Its Dark typing and stat distribution (ATK 70) make it a strong choice for handiwork tasks.
Can you ride Depresso?
Yes, Depresso can be ridden as a ground mount. Craft its saddle at a Pal Gear Workbench. While not a flyer, it provides solid ground mobility.
What does Depresso's Partner Skill do?
Depresso's Partner Skill is Caffeine Inoculation: When activated, Depresso drinks a massive amount of energy drinks, causing its movement speed to increase for a limited time.
Is Depresso good for handiwork?
Depresso has Handiwork Lv 1 — basic capability. It can do the job in a pinch, but there are much stronger options for dedicated handiwork work.
