❓ Frequently Asked Questions — Paladius

How do I get Paladius in Palworld?
Paladius can be caught in the wild. A field boss spawns at Paladius's Domain. It can also be obtained through breeding.
What is Paladius best used for?
Paladius excels at lumbering (Lv 2), Mining (Lv 2). Its Neutral typing and stat distribution (ATK 120) make it a strong choice for lumbering tasks.
Can you ride Paladius?
Yes, Paladius can be ridden as a ground mount. Craft its saddle at a Pal Gear Workbench. While not a flyer, it provides solid ground mobility.
What does Paladius's Partner Skill do?
Paladius's Partner Skill is Holy Knight of the Firmament: Can be ridden. Can triple jump while mounted.
Is Paladius good for lumbering?
It's decent. Paladius has lumbering Lv 2. Serviceable for lumbering if you don't have higher-tier alternatives yet.
