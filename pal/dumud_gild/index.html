❓ Frequently Asked Questions — Dumud Gild

How do I get Dumud Gild in Palworld?
Dumud Gild can be caught in the wild. It can also be obtained through breeding using the standard breeding power formula (BP 1000).
What is Dumud Gild best used for?
Dumud Gild excels at Mining (Lv 2), watering (Lv 1), transporting (Lv 1). Its Neutral typing and stat distribution (ATK 75) make it a strong choice for mining tasks.
Can you ride Dumud Gild?
No, Dumud Gild cannot be ridden. However, it can still be assigned to your base as a worker or added to your party — its strength lies in mining work (Lv 2) rather than transportation.
What does Dumud Gild's Partner Skill do?
Dumud Gild's Partner Skill is Golden Harvest: While in team, increases Attack of Ground Pals. Sometimes drops High Quality Pal Oil when assigned to the ranch, with a small chance to drop Gold Coin.
Is Dumud Gild good for mining?
It's decent. Dumud Gild has Mining Lv 2. Serviceable for mining if you don't have higher-tier alternatives yet.
