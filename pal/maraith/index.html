How do I get Maraith in Palworld?
Maraith can be caught in the wild. It can also be obtained through breeding using the standard breeding power formula (BP 1150).
What is Maraith best used for?
Maraith excels at Gathering (Lv 2), Mining (Lv 1). Its Dark typing and stat distribution (ATK 105) make it a strong choice for gathering tasks.

Is Maraith good for gathering?
It's decent. Maraith has Gathering Lv 2. Serviceable for gathering if you don't have higher-tier alternatives yet.
