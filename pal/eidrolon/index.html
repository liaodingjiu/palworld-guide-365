Eidrolon is a Dragon/Dark dual-type flying mount and Alpha field boss in Palworld 1.0. You can find it on the Sunreach Isles — a floating island region southwest of the main map, reachable via flying mount or the portal atop Feybreak Tower at coordinates (-1293, -1669). It spawns as an Alpha Pal southeast of the Waterlily Gorge statue around (-489, -1524). If you'd rather not hunt it, you can breed one: Blazamut + Lyleen at a Breeding Farm with Cake. Its partner skill Pterosaur Release gives +6% Attack and Speed for each Dragon/Dark Pal in your party — in a themed team, it scales to be one of the fastest flyers in the game.

📋 Eidrolon Quick Takeaways

  1. Two ways to get it: Catch the Alpha on Sunreach Isles (-489, -1524) or breed Blazamut + Lyleen with Cake.
  2. Dragon/Dark typing — weak to Ice and Dragon. Bring Azurobe Cryst or Bastigor if fighting it.
  3. Pterosaur Release scales hard — +6% Attack and Speed per Dragon/Dark Pal in party. Full themed team = massive boost.
  4. Eidrolon Ignis variant swaps Dark for Fire — same partner skill bonus for Fire/Dragon Pals instead, plus Kindling Lv 6.
  5. Reach Sunreach Isles by flying mount or teleporting from Feybreak Tower top (-1293, -1669). No need to beat the tower boss — just fly to the roof.
  6. Base role: item transportation — not a top-tier base worker. Its real value is in the air.
  7. Breedable — unlike Panthalus, you can get Eidrolon without fighting it by pairing Blazamut and Lyleen.

How to Get Eidrolon — Both Methods

Method 1: Catch on Sunreach Isles

  1. Reach Sunreach Isles: fly southwest from the main map or go to Feybreak Tower (-1293, -1669), fly to the roof, and interact with the portal.
  2. Find the Alpha patrol: southeast of Waterlily Gorge statue, around coordinates (-489, -1524).
  3. Fight and catch: bring Ice or Dragon-type Pals. Standard Alpha boss — easier than Panthalus.

Method 2: Breed It

Pair Blazamut + Lyleen at a Breeding Farm with a Cake. Use the Breeding Calculator to find alternative parent combos.

Partner Skill — Pterosaur Release

Increases Eidrolon's Attack and Movement Speed by 6% for every Dark and Dragon-type Pal in your party. With 5 matching-type Pals, that's a +30% bonus. This makes Eidrolon uniquely valuable in Dragon/Dark-themed team compositions — it scales from "good" to "one of the best" depending on your party build.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I find Eidrolon in Palworld?
Eidrolon is an Alpha field boss on Sunreach Isles, a floating island region southwest of the main map. It patrols southeast of the Waterlily Gorge statue at roughly (-489, -1524). Reach Sunreach via flying mount or the portal atop Feybreak Tower (-1293, -1669). No need to beat the tower boss — just fly to the roof.
How do I breed Eidrolon?
Breed Blazamut + Lyleen at a Breeding Farm with Cake. Use the Breeding Calculator to find alternative parent combinations if you don't have both.
What type is Eidrolon and what is it weak to?
Standard Eidrolon is Dragon/Dark dual-type, weak to Ice and Dragon attacks. Its Ignis variant is Dragon/Fire. Good counters: Azurobe Cryst, Chillet, Jetragon, Bastigor, Frostallion.
What does Eidrolon's Partner Skill do?
Pterosaur Release: +6% Attack and Movement Speed per Dark/Dragon Pal in your party. This stacks — a full Dragon/Dark party gives +30%. The Ignis variant gives the same bonus but for Fire/Dragon Pals instead.
Is Eidrolon a good flying mount?
Yes — with Pterosaur Release scaling, Eidrolon becomes one of the fastest flyers in a themed party. In a Dragon/Dark team, it can match or exceed Jetragon's speed while also offering strong Dragon/Dark combat attacks. Solo (no team synergy), Jetragon is faster.
What base work can Eidrolon do?
Standard Eidrolon is best for item transportation. Eidrolon Ignis adds Kindling Lv 6 — making it one of the best furnace/cooking Pals in the game. If you need mining or crafting, use other Pals.
How do I get Eidrolon Ignis?
Eidrolon Ignis is in the World Tree area → Corroded Pit (north-northwest). You need to have captured Panthalus and unlocked the World Tree map to access it. It's a later-game acquisition.
Eidrolon vs Jetragon — which flying mount is better?
Solo: Jetragon is faster at base speed. With team synergy: Eidrolon's Pterosaur Release scales +6% per matching Pal. In a full Dragon/Dark team, Eidrolon can match or exceed Jetragon. If you're not running a themed party, Jetragon is simpler. If you are, Eidrolon pulls ahead.
