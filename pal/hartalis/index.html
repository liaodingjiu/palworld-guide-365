❓ Frequently Asked Questions — Hartalis

How do I get Hartalis in Palworld?
Hartalis can be caught in the wild. It can also be obtained through breeding using the standard breeding power formula (BP 1000).
What is Hartalis best used for?
Hartalis excels at lumbering (Lv 4), Gathering (Lv 3). Its Neutral typing and stat distribution (ATK 130) make it a strong choice for lumbering tasks.
Can you ride Hartalis?
Yes, Hartalis can be ridden as a ground mount. Craft its saddle at a Pal Gear Workbench. While not a flyer, it provides solid ground mobility.
What does Hartalis's Partner Skill do?
Hartalis's Partner Skill is Sacred Barrier: Can be ridden. While mounted, generates a barrier that blocks all attacks. Can triple jump while mounted.
Is Hartalis good for lumbering?
Yes, absolutely. Hartalis has lumbering Lv 4 — top-tier efficiency for this role. It's one of the best Pals you can assign for lumbering tasks.
