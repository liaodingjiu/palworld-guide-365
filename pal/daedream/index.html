How do I get Daedream in Palworld?
Daedream can be caught in the wild. It can also be obtained through breeding using the standard breeding power formula (BP 1230).
What is Daedream best used for?
Daedream excels at Handiwork (Lv 1), Gathering (Lv 1), transporting (Lv 1). Its Dark typing and stat distribution (ATK 75) make it a strong choice for handiwork tasks.
Can you ride Daedream?
No, Daedream cannot be ridden. However, it can still be assigned to your base as a worker or added to your party — its strength lies in handiwork work (Lv 1) rather than transportation.
What does Daedream's Partner Skill do?

Is Daedream good for handiwork?
Daedream has Handiwork Lv 1 — basic capability. It can do the job in a pinch, but there are much stronger options for dedicated handiwork work.
