🧬 How Palworld Breeding Works

Breeding in Palworld follows a simple but powerful system:

  1. Build a Breeding Farm — unlocked at level 19 in the Technology tree.
  2. Place one male and one female Pal — assign them to the Breeding Farm. They must be opposite genders.
  3. Provide Cake — place Cake in the Breeding Farm's chest. One Cake = one egg.
  4. Wait for the egg — a progress bar fills up. When complete, collect the egg.
  5. Hatch the egg — place it in an Egg Incubator and wait for it to hatch.

Two Types of Breeding Outcomes

1. Special Combos (this page): 53 parent pairs that always produce a specific child. These are hardcoded and override the formula.

2. Breeding Power Formula: For all other pairings, the child is determined by averaging the parents' Breeding Power (BP) values. The Pal whose BP is closest to the average is the result.

📐 Breeding Power Formula Explained

Every Pal has a hidden Breeding Power (BP) stat. When you breed two Pals that don't have a special combo:

  1. The game calculates: Average BP = (Parent A BP + Parent B BP) / 2
  2. It finds the Pal whose BP is closest to this average (rounding down)
  3. That Pal becomes the child

Example

Breeding Lamball (BP 1470) with Cattiva (BP 1460):

(1470 + 1460) / 2 = 1465 → Closest BP Pal = Chikipi (BP 1500?) → Check calculator for exact result

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How does Palworld breeding work?
Palworld breeding uses a Breeding Power (BP) system. Each Pal has a hidden BP value. When you breed two Pals, the game averages their BP values and produces the Pal whose BP is closest to that average. Special combos (53 verified on this page) are guaranteed to produce a specific result regardless of the BP calculation. You need a Breeding Farm (unlocked at level 19), one male and one female Pal, and Cake.
What are special breeding combos?
Special breeding combos are parent pairs that guarantee a specific child, bypassing the breeding power formula entirely. For example, Blazehowl + Felbat always produces Blazehowl Noct. These 53 combos are listed in the chart above — they're the only breeding outcomes in Palworld that are 100% predictable without using the breeding power formula.
How many breeding combinations are there in Palworld?
With 215 Pals in Palworld 1.0, there are over 23,000 possible breeding combinations (215 × 214 ÷ 2 unique unordered pairs). Of these, only 53 are special combos that guarantee a specific result. The remaining 23,000+ follow the breeding power formula. Use our Breeding Calculator to compute any pairing.
Can I breed legendary Pals?
Yes, legendary Pals can be bred, but they are extremely difficult because their Breeding Power values are very low (typically 1-200). You'll need to breed Pals whose BP averages near the legendary's BP. For example, two rare/epic Pals might produce a legendary if their average BP is low enough. Check our Breeding Calculator with the legendary Pal as the target to find possible parent combinations.
What do I need for breeding in Palworld?
You need: (1) a Breeding Farm (unlocked at level 19 in the Technology tree), (2) one male and one female Pal (any two Pals of opposite gender), (3) Cake (crafted from Flour, Red Berries, Milk, Egg, and Honey at a Cooking Pot). Place the Pals in the Breeding Farm and put Cake in the farm's chest to start breeding.
