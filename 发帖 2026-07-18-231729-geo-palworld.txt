  ---
  REL.LINK：不建议，三个理由

  1. 时机完全不对

  你站现在 12 天、11% 收录、0 外链、0 自然流量。Guest post 市场卖的是「有权威的
  站给你一条链接」。但反过来看——一个正常运营的博客，凭什么给一个 12 天的新站发
  guest post？要么站主根本不在乎质量（链接农场），要么平台在中间作假（PBN 伪装成
  guest post）。两种情况 Google 都能查出来。

  2. Link buying 是 Google 最大的红线

  Google 对付费外链的态度非常明确——必须加 rel="nofollow" 或
  rel="sponsored"。一旦加了这两个标签，PageRank
  不传递，等于白花钱。如果不加，就是黑帽，查出来整站清空收录。12
  天的新站，Google 一抓一个准——零权威的站突然冒出 N 条 dofollow
  外链，算法直接标记。

  3. 你现在不缺 guest post，缺的是「被 Google 发现」

  对照一下[[geo-optimization]]里的外链策略优先级表：

  ┌─────────────────┬──────────────────────────────────────┬──────────┐
  │     优先级      │                 策略                 │ 你的进度 │

⏺ ModDB & GameBanana — 说实话，不太适合你现在的情况

  ┌───────────────────┬───────────────────────────┬──────────────────────────┐
  │                   │           ModDB           │        GameBanana        │
  ├───────────────────┼───────────────────────────┼──────────────────────────┤
  │ 核心功能          │ 上传 mod 文件、发布 mod   │ 上传 mod                 │
  │                   │ 开发日志                  │ 文件、皮肤、模型         │
  ├───────────────────┼───────────────────────────┼──────────────────────────┤
  │ 能不能发文章      │ ✅ 可以，但要关联一个 mod │ ⚠️ 只能传                │
  │                   │  项目                     │ mod，没有独立文章区      │
  ├───────────────────┼───────────────────────────┼──────────────────────────┤
  │ 纯链接/攻略能发吗 │ ❌ 会删，必须是 mod       │ ❌ 同上                  │
  │                   │ 开发相关内容              │                          │
  ├───────────────────┼───────────────────────────┼──────────────────────────┤
  │ 适合你吗          │ ❌ 你又不是 mod 作者      │ ❌ 同上                  │
  └───────────────────┴───────────────────────────┴──────────────────────────┘

  唯一的用法：把你的 mods.html 包装成 mod 合集发布

  如果你真想用这两个站，只能这样操作：

  1. 在 ModDB 上注册
  2. 创建一个 "mod" 项目（比如 "Palworld 1.0 Essential Mod Collection"）
  3. 项目描述里写 mod 推荐列表（你 mods.html 的内容）
  4. 文末放 "Full updated list → palworldguides.com/mods.html"
  5. 不上传任何文件，纯粹当一个 curated list 发

  但说实话，转化率极低——来这两个站的人是想下载 mod
  的，不是看攻略的。他们看到你页面没有下载按钮直接关掉。

  I Read All 27 Pages of the Palworld 1.0 Patch Notes — Here's What Actually
  Matters

  Subtitle:

  72 new Pals, map doubled, breeding is completely different, and yes you should
  restart

  Body:

  Palworld 1.0 dropped July 10 and the patch notes were twenty seven pages.
  Twenty seven. I went through all of it so you don't have to.

  Here's what you actually need to know.

  The Big Stuff

  72 new Pals. That's 47 brand new species plus 25 variants. Total Paldeck is
  287 now. Most of them live in two new zones — Sunreach (floating islands you
  can reach around level 45) and the World Tree (the endgame area people have
  been asking about since the first trailer).

  Level cap is 80 now, up from 65. Condensation used to need 116 Pals to max
  out, now it's 48. Small change, huge time save.

  Breeding Got a Full Rework

  This is the part that tripped up a lot of returning players. Old breeding
  combos from early access don't work anymore. The formula got adjusted. If you
  have a breeding chart bookmarked from 2024 or 2025, delete it.

  There's also a whole new mutation system now. Use a Special Cake instead of a
  normal one in the Breeding Farm, and each egg has a 5-10% shot at hatching a
  mutated Pal with passives you literally can't get any other way. There are 4
  new cake types total, each doing something different — better stats, twin
  eggs, higher mutation odds, better passive inheritance.

  You Can Build on Water Now

  Repair kits are gone. Your Pals auto-repair structures for free. You can build
  bases on water with the Aquatic Construction Kit. Wave-based raids replaced
  the old random raid system. Turrets no longer eat ammo. Honestly the base
  building changes alone are worth the update.

  New Weapons, New Combat

  13 new weapons from early game melee to endgame energy stuff — Laser Sword,
  Plasma Rifle, Drone Launcher, Beam Launcher. You can equip 6 weapons at once
  now. Partner skill stacking got removed so you can't just run 5 Jetragons
  anymore. You actually need a diverse team.

  Should You Restart?

  Pocketpair says yes. Old saves technically load but progression, spawn
  locations, story beats — all of it got reworked. Your old Jetragon army isn't
  going to carry over the way you think. Keep the old save, start a new
  character.

  If you want the full breakdown with breeding calculator, Pal database, and
  interactive tools, I've been building a resource here: palworldguides.com

  It's free, no ads yet, just stuff I wished existed when I started playing.
