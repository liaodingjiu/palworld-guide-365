📌 Key Takeaways

  • Ancient Civilization Cores drop from combat only — you cannot buy them, mine them, or craft them. You must fight.
  • The Alpha Pal circuit is the most efficient farm — chain 10-15 Alpha Pal kills per in-game day for 15-40+ cores per cycle.
  • All sources respawn — Alpha Pals reset after 1 in-game day (~30 min), towers can be replayed instantly, dungeons refresh periodically.
  • Start farming early — you'll need 50+ cores to unlock and craft all Ancient Technology items by endgame.
  • Bring a fast flying mount — the faster you travel between Alpha spawns, the more cores per hour you earn.

💎 What Are Ancient Civilization Cores?

Ancient Civilization Cores are rare crafting materials obtained by defeating powerful enemies in Palworld. They represent remnants of the ancient civilization that created the Pals — hence the name — and are used to unlock and craft Ancient Technology items.

Unlike most resources, cores cannot be mined, gathered, or purchased. The only way to get them is through combat. This makes them one of the few truly "unskippable" grinds in Palworld.

You'll first encounter them around level 15-20 when you start killing Alpha Pals, and you'll continue needing them through the endgame for Legendary-tier gear.

💡 Did you know? Ancient Civilization Cores share the same icon as Ancient Civilization Parts — but they're different items. Parts drop from lower-tier enemies and are used for basic Ancient Tech. Cores are the rarer, endgame version.

🗺️ Alpha Pal Farming Route

The most reliable and sustainable method. Alpha Pals are field bosses marked on your map — each drops 1-3 cores per kill.

Recommended Early-Mid Game Alpha Route (Level 15-30)

This route hits 8 Alphas with minimal travel time. Fly a loop and collect 10-20 cores per run.

  1. Chillet (Lv 11) — Grassy Behemoth Hills. Easy kill, 1-2 cores.
  2. Swee (Lv 13) — Windswept Hills. Fast kill. 1 core.
  3. Penking (Lv 15) — Sealed Realm of the Frozen Wing. 1-2 cores.
  4. Gumoss (Lv 11) — Grassy Behemoth Hills. Very easy. 1 core.
  5. Azurobe (Lv 17) — Sealed Realm of the Lake. 1-2 cores.
  6. Katress (Lv 23) — Sealed Realm of the Dark Moon. 1-2 cores.
  7. Bushi (Lv 23) — Sealed Realm of the Sword. 1-2 cores.
  8. Elphidran (Lv 26) — Sealed Realm of the Dragon. 2-3 cores.

Advanced Route (Level 35+, Higher Yield)

Add these to the rotation once you have a strong combat team and a fast flying mount like Jetragon or Faleris:

  • Petallia (Lv 28) — 2-3 cores
  • Warsect (Lv 30) — 2-3 cores
  • Menasting (Lv 36) — 2-3 cores
  • Anubis (Lv 42) — 2-4 cores
  • Jormuntide (Lv 45) — 2-4 cores
  • Astegon (Lv 48) — 3-4 cores
  • Jetragon (Lv 50) — 3-5 cores
  • Frostallion (Lv 50) — 3-5 cores
🗺️ Pro tip: Use our interactive map to plan your Alpha Pal route. After completing one full circuit, wait one in-game day (or sleep in a bed) and all Alphas will have respawned.

🏰 Tower Boss Farming

Tower bosses drop 3-5 cores per clear and can be replayed instantly — no respawn wait.

Tower bosses are the second-best farming source. The downside is they're tough fights with a 5-minute time limit in Palworld 1.0. But if you can clear them consistently, they offer the highest cores-per-minute in the game.

🏚️ Dungeon & Chest Farming

Dungeons are less efficient than Alpha routes but good for variety and extra loot while leveling.

How It Works

  • Dungeon chests (the gold ones at the end of each dungeon) have a 40-60% chance to contain 1-3 Ancient Civilization Cores in level 40+ dungeons.
  • Dungeons are marked on the map — look for cave entrance icons. Higher-level areas (Mount Obsidian, Astral Mountains, Dessicated Desert) have better drop rates.
  • Dungeons respawn periodically — the same dungeon entrance won't always be active. Fly around and check multiple locations.
⚠️ Dungeon farming is inconsistent. If you're purely chasing cores, stick to Alpha Pals and tower bosses. Use dungeons as a bonus while leveling or exploring — not as your primary farming method.

⚔️ Raid Boss Drops

Endgame raid bosses drop the most cores per kill but require coordinated groups.

Raid bosses (Bellanoir, Blazamut Ryu, Xenolord) drop 5-8 Ancient Civilization Cores per successful raid. These are by far the most efficient per-kill source — but they require:

  • A Raid Summon Altar at your base (crafted with Ancient Technology points)
  • Slab fragments to summon the boss (dropped by high-level Pals and dungeon chests)
  • A well-prepared base with defensive structures and a strong Pal team
  • Multiple players recommended — raid bosses are balanced for groups

If you have a dedicated group farming raid bosses, you'll accumulate cores faster than any other method. For solo players, stick with the Alpha Pal circuit.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do you get Ancient Civilization Cores in Palworld?
Ancient Civilization Cores drop from Alpha Pals (field bosses), tower bosses, dungeon chests, and raid bosses. The most reliable method is farming Alpha Pals on a rotation — each drops 1-3 cores and respawns after one in-game day (~30 minutes). Tower bosses drop 3-5 cores per clear and can be replayed instantly.
What are Ancient Civilization Cores used for?
Cores are used to craft Ancient Technology items: Pal Essence Condenser, Egg Incubator, Grappling Guns, Shield Generators, high-tier Spheres (Hyper/Ultra/Legendary), and boss-themed weapons. You'll need 40+ cores to unlock and craft everything.
What is the fastest way to farm Ancient Civilization Cores?
Combine three sources: (1) Alpha Pal circuit — hit 10-15 Alphas in a loop (15-40+ cores per cycle), (2) speedrun tower bosses for 3-5 cores each, (3) raid bosses for 5-8 cores each. An optimized route yields 30-50 cores per hour.
Do Ancient Civilization Cores respawn?
Yes. Alpha Pals respawn every in-game day (~30 real minutes). Tower bosses can be re-fought instantly. Dungeon chests reset when the dungeon refreshes. Raid bosses can be re-summoned as long as you have the slab fragments. Cores are a fully renewable resource.
Can you buy Ancient Civilization Cores?
No. Ancient Civilization Cores cannot be purchased from any vendor. They are exclusively obtained through combat — defeating Alpha Pals, clearing tower bosses, looting dungeon chests, and completing raids.
