How Palworld Breeding Works

Every Pal has a hidden breeding power value (90–1500). When two Pals breed, the game averages their values and produces the Pal closest to that average. For example: Anubis (570) + Bushi (1120) → 845 → the Pal closest to 845 is the child. You need a Breeding Farm (level 19) and Cake. One Cake lasts for multiple cycles — automate Milk, Eggs, and Honey at your Ranch for a steady supply.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How does Palworld breeding work?
Each Pal has a hidden breeding power value (90–1500). When two Pals breed, the game averages their values and produces the Pal closest to that average. You need a Breeding Farm (level 19) and Cake.
Can I breed Legendary Pals like Jetragon?
No. Jetragon, Frostallion, Paladius, and Necromus are genderless and cannot be bred. However, variants like Frostallion Noct (Frostallion + Helzephyr) CAN be bred.
What is the formula for breeding in Palworld?
The formula is: (Parent A breeding power + Parent B breeding power) ÷ 2 → find the Pal with the closest breeding power. Example: Anubis (570) + Bushi (1120) = 845 → closest Pal is the child.
How long does breeding take?
Breeding takes about 5 minutes. Incubation: ~20 min (regular), ~45 min (large), up to 2 hours (huge). Adjust timers in world/server settings.
What items do I need to breed Pals?
You need a Breeding Farm (level 19) and Cake (Flour + Red Berries + Milk + Egg + Honey). Automate Milk (Mozzarina), Eggs (Chikipi), Honey (Elizabee) at your Ranch.
