❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How is the Palworld tier list calculated?
Combat tier = Attack stat. Base Work tier = total work suitability levels. Breeding tier = breeding power (lower BP = rarer = better). S=top 10%, A=10-25%, B=25-45%, C=45-70%, D=rest. All data from palworld.wiki.gg, updated for 1.0.
What is the strongest Pal in Palworld?
Bellanoir Libero and Bellanoir have the highest attack (150). Necromus and Neptilius follow at 145. Jetragon (140) is top-tier for speed and flying. Switch to the Combat tab above to see the full S-Tier ranking.
Which Pal should I use for base work?
Switch to the Base Work tab — it ranks Pals by total work suitability. Anubis (Handiwork Lv4 + Mining Lv3) and Jormuntide Ignis (Kindling Lv4) consistently rank S-Tier for base tasks. For specific jobs, check the individual Pal pages for exact work levels.
What's the difference between Combat, Base Work, and Breeding tiers?
Combat ranks by raw Attack stat — best for fighting and tower bosses. Base Work ranks by combined work suitability levels — best for your base. Breeding ranks by breeding power (lower = rarer = harder to breed) — best for breeding chains and value. A Pal can be S-Tier in one category and C-Tier in another.
Is this tier list updated for Palworld 1.0?
Yes — all 215 Pals including 1.0 additions are ranked. Data is sourced from palworld.wiki.gg and reflects the current 1.0 stats. The catch bonus now caps at 5 per species, so breeding for top-tier stats matters more than ever for S-Tier Pals.
Should I only use S-Tier Pals?
No. S-Tier Pals are the best in their category, but A-Tier and B-Tier Pals are still very strong and often easier to obtain. Early game, you won't have access to S-Tier Pals — focus on catching what's available and upgrading over time. A well-bred B-Tier Pal can outperform a poorly-bred S-Tier Pal.
How often is this tier list updated?
The tier list is recalculated whenever new game data is available — typically within a week of a major Palworld update. Rankings are generated automatically from wiki.gg stats, so they reflect the current game version without manual bias.
Where can I find specific Pal stats and breeding combos?
Click any Pal card in the tier list to go to its full detail page, or browse the Pal Database. For breeding combinations, use the Breeding Calculator to find parent pairs for any target Pal.
